ABOUT US

Knovva Academy is a cross-cultural, innovative education company located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. ​ ​

With professors and scholars from Harvard Education School and Harvard Kennedy School as advisors and core team members, Knovva Academy provides top-quality immersive project learning experiences in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics ) subjects for junior and senior high school students all over the world.

Recently invested by an overseas venture capital, we project to bring more technology experts on board to reinforce our core competency - immersive project-based learning experience, using cutting-edge technologies including virtual reality, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

We believe balanced knowledge base and life-long learning form the impetus for all social progress. And we want to leverage the technology breakthroughs to entice students' interests to achieve them.
